ENTRANCE THE SHOP THE FITTING ROOM THE SITE THE STREETS

THE IDEA

Engage people in a dynamic that can be summarized as 'the more original you can be the more discounts you obtain'

the shop

Take a maximum of 3 different clothes you wish to combine. Top? Skirt? Hat?

the fitting room

Try all the clothes together, choose a nice background, get your smartphone ready, and shoot a good one on Instagram! Don't forget to add the corresponding hashtag for each article.
















We choose Instagram as the main media because it's a
social network with over 30 million users all around the world
and because it's a fresh one, not been used for the advertising by a lot of brands

the site

Check the internet, your cool fashion picture is on the move!

How many people tried the same hat you did? How many shot themselves wearing the same jacket? The less coincidences the more creative you are, and therefore the more discount you obtain to buy that pack of clothes on your picture.

the streets

Who was the biggest discount of the week for? Was it you? Then your photograph will appear in several streets of the city. That's right, you just became our model for a week! What's gonna be the new style on sunday?
